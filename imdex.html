heyy toosha

she is my world, my beginning and end,
my greatest adventure, my very best friend

the world is a shadow when compared to you
the truest of blessings i ever once knew

pure is the voice that she does own
the sweetest comfort that ive ever known

the moon looks down from up above
admiring her with silent love

the stars all talk as she pass
like diamonds falling on a glass

her walk is just like the midnight air,
with silent beauty, pretty and rare

the night sky is huge and deep
a quiet space where angels sleep

but all its stars would fade from view,
the moment my gaze drifts to you.

Every word of this humble poem flew directly from my heart, carrying my love for you. I poured my entire heart into every single letter.

Happy 6 months of loving you.
Eid Mubarak my angel, my Rahaf, my everything.
